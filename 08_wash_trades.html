🚨 "Tiven S.A. existiert nicht"

🚨 „Tiven S.A. existiert nicht"

⚠️ Critical Finding — Verified in Source Data
"Tiven S.A. existiert nicht."
„Tiven S.A. existiert nicht."
The company's own records contain this explicit warning: "Tiven S.A. does not exist." The note clarifies that what is labelled "Tiven S.A." is actually "Tiven Sàrl" — a different legal entity type entirely. Yet on 5 August 2025, exactly 244,445 shares (4.38% of SyncPilot) were transferred to this entity, making it the 7th largest shareholder. At the prevailing €23 per share, these shares carry a notional value of approximately €5.6 million.
Die unternehmenseigenen Unterlagen enthalten diese ausdrückliche Warnung: „Tiven S.A. existiert nicht." Die Anmerkung stellt klar, dass es sich bei der als „Tiven S.A." bezeichneten Gesellschaft tatsächlich um eine „Tiven Sàrl" handelt — ein völlig anderer Rechtsformtyp. Dennoch wurden am 5. August 2025 genau 244.445 Aktien (4,38% von SyncPilot) auf diese Gesellschaft übertragen, was sie zur siebtgrößten Aktionärin macht. Beim vorherrschenden Kurs von €23 pro Aktie haben diese Aktien einen Nominalwert von ca. 5,6 Mio. €.

⚡ The December 31 Blitz: 20 Transfers, One Day

⚡ Der 31.-Dezember-Blitz: 20 Übertragungen an einem Tag

On 31 December 2023, twenty separate share transfers were executed simultaneously — the single largest restructuring event in SyncPilot's history. Every transfer was authorized under "Anlage 2 Niederschrift Hauptversammlung" (Appendix 2 of the General Meeting minutes). The transfers moved shares from the original founding vehicles (BOSYNC, AURIGA, DSI, Augusta, H.G. Perseus) to a new constellation of entities — many of which were created shortly before.

Am 31. Dezember 2023 wurden zwanzig separate Aktienübertragungen gleichzeitig durchgeführt — die größte einzelne Umstrukturierung in der Geschichte von SyncPilot. Jede Übertragung wurde gemäß „Anlage 2 Niederschrift Hauptversammlung" genehmigt.

Applicable Legal Framework

Anwendbarer Rechtsrahmen

§ 20 AktG
Disclosure of Holdings
Mitteilungspflichten
Enterprises holding more than 25% of shares must notify the company. Multi-layered holding structures may circumvent this obligation if individual entities stay below threshold.
§ 117 AktG
Liability for Undue Influence
Haftung für unzulässige Einflussnahme
Anyone who uses their influence on the company to induce board members or supervisory board members to act to the detriment of the company or its shareholders is liable for damages.
§ 71a AktG
Prohibition of Concealed Acquisition
Verbot der verdeckten Einlagenrückgewähr
Legal transactions circumventing restrictions on a company's acquisition of its own shares are void. Transfers between entities under common control may constitute concealed self-dealing.
§ 263 StGB
Betrug / Fraud
Betrug
If circular transfers were designed to create the appearance of arm's-length transactions to deceive investors or regulators, they may constitute fraud under German criminal law.
GwG §§ 3, 10
Anti-Money Laundering
Geldwäschegesetz
The complexity of the ownership structure — 30+ entities, 4+ jurisdictions, non-existent entities — may trigger due diligence obligations under the German Money Laundering Act.
§ 185 AktG
Share Issuance Requirements
Anforderungen an die Aktienausgabe
Shares must be issued to entities with legal personality. If "Tiven S.A." has no legal existence, any share issuance or transfer to it may be legally void (nichtig).
